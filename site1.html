About FUATECH

A New Era of Agriculture & Technology Research Institute in Nigeria

The Federal University of Agriculture & Technology (FUATECH), Obio Akpa is aimed at problem-solving teaching and research in both food production systems and cutting-edge technology, to create business, sustainability, and societal needs. Building a globally significant citadel of learning with highest quality research, teaching and collaboration while engaging locally, nationally and with government to solve Nigeria's problems.

Mission

To advance agricultural transformation through cutting-edge research, innovation, teaching and the application of Smart Technologies, producing globally competitive Graduates and sustainable solutions for food security and economic development.

Vision

To be one of the foremost universities in Africa promoting smart, innovation and technology-driven Agriculture and AgriBusiness.
